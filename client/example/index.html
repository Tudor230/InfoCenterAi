Library quiet hours policy

Started 2 minutes ago

What hours do the main library quiet floors observe during finals week?

Finals-week quiet hours on Levels 2–4 of the Whitaker Learning Commons run from 8:00 a.m.–midnight, Monday through Thursday, with extended silent study until 2:00 a.m. on Friday and Saturday. Staff monitor the floors every 30 minutes, and white-noise machines switch on at 6:00 p.m. to help keep conversations at a whisper.

Group rooms remain available 24/7 on Level 1. Students can book a slot in the Campus Spaces portal, but noise must stay contained inside each room per Residential Life guidance.
